Welcome to RCB Team's management page!

Here's a guide to the website:

  • HOME

    Brings you back to this page from wherever you are!

  • Matches

    Displays the list of matches our team has played and also the details of each match.

  • Player

    Displays the list of all of our team's players and their personal details.

  • Batsman

    Displays the list of professional statistics of our batters!

  • Bowler

    Displays the list of professional statistics of our bowlers!

  • Auction

    Displays the details of the price in $ at which our players were bought or sold!

  • Venue

    Displays the list of all the venues at which our team's matches are held

  • Stored Procedure 1

    For given player name, it displays the details of that particular player in Player and Batsman table.

  • Stored Procedure 2

    For given player name, it displays the details of that particular player in Player and Bowler table.
